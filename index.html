Our Memory Path

The journey of our hearts becoming one

First Date

The Day Everything Changed

It was a beautiful sunny day after rain when our eyes first met. I picked you up at the train station, and we went to a coffee shop at North Jakarta. I still remember the butterflies in my stomach and the way you smiled at me. It felt like the universe conspired to bring us together. The world seemed to stop, and all I could see was you.

We also went to PIk and had a wonderful time by driving electric scooter around the area. We shared stories, laughter, and a connection that felt so natural. That day marked the beginning of our beautiful journey together. I knew from that moment that you were someone special, and I wanted to know you better.

Second Date

Travel to Your Place

Our second date was a memorable one. I traveled to your place, met your parents and family, and we spent the day exploring the city together. We visited the mall, had lunch at a cozy restaurant, spending time at coffee shop and shared so many laughs. Took a lot of photos together, capturing the moments we shared. It was a day filled with joy and happiness, and I felt so lucky to have you by my side.

First "I Love You"

Three Little Words

After waking up from a sleep, I realized how much you meant to me. I couldn't hold back my feelings any longer, and I finally found the courage to tell you those three words that had been burning in my heart. Your reaction was priceless, even though it was just through a chat. You smiled and looked more energized after hearing those words. It was a moment I will never forget, and it made me fall in love with you even more.

Family Trip

Car Free Day

Our first family trip together was a day filled with laughter and love. We woke up early, ready to explore the Purwakarta city on a car free day. We had a delicious breakfast "Soto Ayam", sharing stories and making memories.

We also visited the beautiful Jatiluhur Dam, enjoying the scenic views and taking lots of photos. The weather was perfect, and we enjoyed the fresh air as we walked hand in hand. It was a day that brought us closer together, and I felt so grateful to have you in my life.

Memorable City

Bandung

Our trip to Bandung was a special one. We explored the city, visited beautiful places, and created unforgettable memories. The weather was perfect, and we enjoyed every moment together. We took lots of photos, capturing the beauty of the city and our love for each other.

We also had a wonderful time at the Kawah Putih, enjoying the stunning views and taking in the fresh air. It was a day filled with laughter, love, and adventure. Our trip to Bandung will always hold a special place in my heart.

Situ Patenggang was a highlight of our trip. The serene lake and the surrounding tea plantations created a magical atmosphere. Cold breeze, the sound of nature, and the beauty of the place made it a perfect spot for us to relax and enjoy each other's company.

I cherish every moment we spent together, and I look forward to many more adventures with you. You are my love, my partner, and my best friend. I can't wait to see what the future holds for us.

Family Gathering

Loji

Enjoying curug bandung with her family was a delightful experience. The sound of the water cascading down the rocks was soothing, and the lush greenery surrounding us created a peaceful atmosphere. We spent quality time together, sharing stories, and laughter as we took in the beauty of nature.

First Movie Date

Sore: Istri dari Masa Depan

Watching "Sore: Istri dari Masa Depan" together was a memorable experience. The film's storyline resonated with us. We shared our thoughts and feelings about the characters, and it sparked some deep conversations between us.
